I have been lucky enough to gain experience in the cannabis industry over the past 8 years. I now run a wonderful program out of a dispensary in Santa Cruz called the Compassion Program. My mother is an MD and natural doctor who is one of the leading cannabis doctors in California. I feel that after graduating I will have a lot of opportunity to progress in the industry due to the connections I've developed and medical consultation skills I have learned.

The Compassion Program has been designed to provide free cannabis medicine to low-income patients. In addition to receiving free medication, I work with each patient individually to help him or her find out what products work best for them. All products are donated at no cost from local vendors. I started the program in mid-2014 when KindPeoples opened. I truly believe I make a strong impact in the lives of the patients I work with and would not trade the job for anything. In a way the program now directly represents my ability to contribute to the community, and I plan to continue to work hard.
